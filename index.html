About the Event

The VSSUT Marathon is a premier running event dedicated to promoting health, fitness, and a drug-free lifestyle. Join thousands of participants from across Odisha as we run together for a better tomorrow.

Date: December 15, 2023

Location: VSSUT Campus, Burla, Odisha

Categories: 5KM, 10KM, and Half Marathon (21.1KM)

Registration Fee: Rs. 200 for non-VSSUT participants (VSSUT students/staff: College-sponsored)

Why Participate?

  • Health Benefits: Improve cardiovascular health and overall fitness
  • Community Support: Join a supportive community promoting drug-free living
  • Personal Achievement: Challenge yourself and achieve new milestones
  • Free T-Shirt & Medal: Get commemorative items for all finishers
  • Medical Support: Professional medical teams along the route
  • Refreshments: Water stations and energy drinks throughout the course
